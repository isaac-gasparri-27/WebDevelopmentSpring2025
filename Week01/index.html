Meatball Sub

Meatball subs are a great lunchtime or dinnertime meal! The warm roll, melty cheese, and tasty meatballs make for an unforgettable experience in your mouth! This recipe is for 4 sandwiches and takes about 40 minutes total, great for a family meal!

Ingredients:

  • Baguettes
  • Ground Beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Parmesian
  • Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Provalone Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Make the Meatballs: Mix the ground beef, bread crumbs, eggs, garlic, Parmesan, and spices with your hands. Shape into evenly sized meatballs and bake until they reach an internal temperature of 160 degrees F.
  2. Prep the Bread: Cut the baguette in half (lengthwise) and scoop out some of the bread to make room for the meatballs. Brush olive oil over the bread and sprinkle with seasonings. Bake in the oven until lightly toasted.
  3. Make the Sauce: Heat a jar of spaghetti sauce on the stove. Once cooked, transfer the meatballs to the sauce and mix them until coated.
  4. Assemble the Sandwich: Spoon meatballs and sauce into the baguette. Top with provolone cheese. Bake the entire sandwich until the cheese is melted.
